Quatre Project Management Services Limited is a knowledge-based firm engaged in the provision of Project Management and Engineering Consultancy Services and Solutions particularly in the Built Environment. As an ISO Certified firm, Quatre Project Management Services Limited has strong expertise in the provision of reliable knowledge based engineering and project management services focusing on key engineering areas of civil engineering and building, electrical, mechanical/industrial, environmental, transportation and energy using state-of-the-art competencies, and in full compliance of company core values on one hand and the national, regional and international standards on the other.
